Can you run the whole coffee shop on your own?


My coffee Maker cafe life is for all the crazy coffee lovers out there who can think to start their day without having a cup of coffee from their favorite spot. As a true coffee maker shop owner, you will get to meet your customers from various backgrounds and get to help them navigate through life.
